Privacy Policy

Last updated 2026-06-03 · Effective 2026-06-03

This Privacy Policy describes how the Leap37 iOS app ("Leap37", "the app", "we") handles information when you use it. Leap37 is an independent personal project that helps you decide when to stop looking and commit to a choice, using the 37% rule from optimal-stopping theory.

In short: Leap37 collects no data about you, contains no analytics or advertising, makes no network connections, and sends nothing to us or any third party. Everything you enter stays on your device.

What we do not collect

Leap37 does not collect:

  • Your name, email, phone number, postal address, or any other contact info.
  • Your precise or coarse location.
  • Your photos, contacts, microphone, camera, calendar, health, or motion data.
  • Your device's advertising identifier (IDFA), unique device identifier (IDFV), or fingerprint.
  • Usage, browsing, or interaction analytics. The app contains no analytics SDK, no crash-reporting SDK, no advertising SDK, and no marketing or attribution SDK.

What is stored on your device

Leap37 stores the following on-device only, via Apple's standard UserDefaults:

  • The decisions you create — their titles, the number of options, the candidates and ratings you add, and which option you commit to.
  • Basic in-app preferences.

This information never leaves your device, is not readable by us, and is permanently removed when you delete the app.

No network connections

Leap37 works fully offline. It does not contact any server, does not load remote content, and does not transmit your decisions or any other information over the internet. The only time the app opens a network connection is if you tap one of the links on the About screen (this Privacy Policy, the Terms of Use, or the contact email), which open in your browser or mail app outside of Leap37.

Third parties

Leap37 integrates no third-party services, SDKs, or trackers. We do not share, sell, rent, or otherwise disclose any information to advertisers, data brokers, or analytics providers — because there is nothing to share.

Children

Leap37 is a general-audience app suitable for all ages. It is not directed at children and we do not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children.

Your rights

Because Leap37 does not collect personally identifying information, there is no personal record for us to access, correct, or delete. To remove everything the app has stored on your device, uninstall it via iOS Settings → General → iPhone Storage → Leap37 → Delete App.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent change. Material changes will be noted in the app's release notes.
