Privacy Policy

Last updated 2026-06-19 · Effective 2026-06-19

This Privacy Policy describes how the Leap37 iOS app ("Leap37", "the app", "we", "us") handles information when you use it. Leap37 is an independent personal project that helps you decide when to stop looking and commit to a choice, using the 37% rule from optimal-stopping theory.

In short: Leap37 works fully offline and keeps your decisions on your device. Signing in is entirely optional. If you choose to sign in, we store your decisions and basic account details on our server only so they sync across your devices and so we can reply to feedback you send. We show no ads, use no analytics, and do not track you.

Using Leap37 without an account

You can use every core feature — the calculator, the templates, and tracking your decisions — without signing in. In this mode the app works fully offline: your decisions are stored only on your device via Apple's standard UserDefaults, are not readable by us, and are removed when you delete the app. Nothing is sent to us or any third party.

Optional accounts and cloud sync

Leap37 offers an optional account so you can back up your decisions and sync them across your devices. You create one with Sign in with Apple or Sign in with Google. If — and only if — you sign in, we collect and store the following on our own server:

  • Account identifier — a stable user ID provided by Apple or Google so we can recognize your account.
  • Name and email address — as supplied by Apple or Google at sign-in (Apple lets you hide your real email behind a private relay address). These identify your account and let us reply if you contact us.
  • Your decisions — the decisions you create and their contents (titles, option counts, candidates, ratings, and committed choices), so they can be restored and kept in sync on your other devices.
  • Feedback you send — if you use the in-app feedback option, the message you write, along with your account name and email so we can follow up.

We use this information solely to provide the sync and feedback features. We do not use it for advertising, profiling, or tracking, and we do not sell or rent it.

What we never collect

  • Your precise or coarse location.
  • Your photos, contacts, microphone, camera, calendar, health, or motion data.
  • Your device's advertising identifier (IDFA) or any cross-app or cross-website tracking identifier.
  • Usage, browsing, or interaction analytics — the app contains no analytics, crash-reporting, advertising, or attribution SDKs.

Third parties

Leap37 relies on a small number of services only to provide the optional account:

  • Sign in with Apple and Sign in with Google handle authentication. When you sign in, the provider gives the app a token confirming your identity. See Apple's Privacy Policy and Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Our server is operated by us on cloud hosting (DigitalOcean) and stores only the account data described above. We do not share it with advertisers, data brokers, or analytics providers.

Data retention and deletion

If you sign in, your account data is kept until you delete it. You can permanently delete your account and the decisions stored on our server at any time from inside the app (Account → Delete account). Signing out stops syncing and leaves your decisions on the device. Feedback messages may be retained after account deletion so we can address the issues you raised. Deleting the app removes everything stored locally on your device.

Security

All communication between the app and our server is encrypted in transit (HTTPS), and your session token is stored in the iOS Keychain. No method of storage or transmission is perfectly secure, but we minimize what we collect and protect it accordingly.

Children

Leap37 is a general-audience app and is not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Your rights

You may access, correct, or delete your account data. The simplest way to delete everything we hold is Account → Delete account in the app. You may also email us with any request. Depending on where you live, you may have additional rights under laws such as the GDPR or CCPA; we honor those requests.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent change. Material changes will be noted in the app's release notes.
