What the app is

Leap37 is an independent personal project that helps you apply the "37% rule" from optimal-stopping theory (the classic "secretary problem") to real-life sequential choices — such as apartment hunting, hiring, shopping, or dating. It calculates when to stop exploring and start committing, and lets you track options as you go. The app is provided free of charge.
