4.  DESCRIPTION (max 4000 characters)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Some choices come at you one at a time — apartments, job candidates, used cars,
even dates — and once you pass on one, it's usually gone. Search too little and
you settle; search too long and the best option slips away. Leap37 solves this
with a result from mathematics called the 37% rule (the "secretary problem").

THE RULE, MADE SIMPLE

Look at the first ~37% of your options without committing — just learn the
field. Then leap: commit to the first option that beats everything you saw while
looking. That strategy gives you the mathematically best shot — about a 37%
chance — at landing the single best option.

WHAT'S INSIDE

• Calculator — Enter how many options you'll see, or a time horizon (days,
  weeks, months, years), and instantly get your look/leap split.
• Decisions — Track real options as you go. Leap37 sets your benchmark from the
  looking phase and tells you the moment you should commit.
• Templates — 25+ ready-made scenarios across home, career, business, dating,
  shopping, travel, and services. Tap one to start in seconds.
• Time horizons — Some decisions play out over years, not items. "Dating for 20
  years" → explore for the first ~7.4 years, then commit. Leap37 does the math.
• Learn — Plain-English explanation of why 37%, when it applies, and how the
  same idea works for any variable: time, number of options, money spent,
  distance traveled, interviews, deals, and more.
• Optional cloud sync — Sign in with Apple or Google to back up your decisions
  and keep them in sync across your iPhone and iPad. Totally optional.

PRIVACY FIRST

Leap37 works without an account and keeps your decisions on your device. Signing
in (Apple or Google) is optional — if you do, your decisions sync across your
devices and back up to the cloud, and you can delete your account and that data
anytime. Leap37 is free and supported by non-personalized ads (Google AdMob); we
don't use other analytics and don't track you across other apps. Read the full
Privacy Policy at https://leetsyplakov.github.io/Leap37-policies/privacy.html.

Leap37 is an independent app and offers general decision-making guidance only —
not professional, financial, legal, or medical advice.

18. APP PREVIEW / PROMO PARAGRAPH (Product Hunt, blog, newsletter)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

We all face choices that come one at a time — apartments that get rented the
same day, job offers with deadlines, dates you can't un-pass. Search too little
and you settle; search too long and the best option is gone. There's actually a
mathematical answer to this, called the 37% rule (or "secretary problem"):
explore the first 37% of your options to learn the field, then commit to the
first one that beats them all.

Leap37 turns that theorem into a simple app. Punch in how many options you'll
see — or a time horizon like "6 months" or "20 years" — and it shows your
look/leap split instantly. Track real options as you go and it tells you the
exact moment to stop and commit. It ships with 25+ templates for the decisions
people actually agonize over. It's free and ad-supported — non-personalized ads,
no cross-app tracking — with an optional free sign-in (Apple or Google) that backs
up and syncs your decisions across your devices.
